THE STORY

Born in Rourkela,
Built for Creators.

We got tired of sterile, clinical studios that felt like hospitals. Music isn't clean. It's messy, emotional, and human.

Rourkela Music Design (RMD) is an experiment in sound. Located in the steel city, we forge audio with the same intensity. Whether you're an indie band, a hip-hop artist, or a storyteller, we provide the gear and the vibe to let you break the rules.
